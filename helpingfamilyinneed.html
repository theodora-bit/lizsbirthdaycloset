Welcome to Liz's closet

Liz's Birthday Closet's mission is to purchase and provide birthday tool kits, containing free birthday gifts and essential party supplies, to less fortunate families in Bucks and Montgomery Counties. We focus on serving children between the ages of 1 and 16, ensuring they have the resources to celebrate their special day.
